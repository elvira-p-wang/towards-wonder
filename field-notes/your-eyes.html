Your Eyes.

No. 009 Perception 3 May 2026 1 min read

II used to think your eyes looked like glacial meltwater.

Clear. Quiet. Beautiful from a distance.

Now I think they are more like a spring, gently warmed by the afternoon sun.

They carry a kind of warmth that feels unusually wholehearted, and a kind of beauty that never asks to be noticed.

Perhaps your eyes never changed.

Only the way I learned to see them did.
