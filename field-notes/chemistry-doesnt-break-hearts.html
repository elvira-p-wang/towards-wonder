Chemistry Doesn't Break Hearts.

No. 002 Humour 6 July 2022 1 min read

AA parody of the Chinese song Because of Love (《因为爱情》), rewritten immediately after a chemistry exam.

I went to Shenzhen Middle School. The chemistry paper, however, was written by Guangya High School. This is important context.

Because of chemistry,

hearts don't break so easily.

Therefore, everything still looks wonderfully fine.

Because of Guangya,

I gaze into the distance.

I could still completely lose my mind because of you.

Because of chemistry,

there could never be sadness.

We were simply... chemically transformed.

Because of Guangya,

somewhere out there,

the people who wrote this paper are probably living long, peaceful, and extremely satisfying lives.
