Chemistry Doesn't Break Hearts.

No. 002 Parody 6 July 2022 1 min read

AA parody of the Chinese song Because of Love (《因为爱情》), rewritten after a chemistry exam.

I attended Shenzhen Middle School. The exam paper was written by Guangya High School.

Because of Chemistry,

Hearts don't break today.

Everything still looks okay.

Because of Guangya,

I slowly drift away.

Wondering what crime I had to pay.

Because of Chemistry,

Love never went astray.

We all got oxidised that day.

Because of Guangya,

Somewhere far away,

The people who wrote this paper are sleeping well today.
