Mathematics Breaks Everything.

No. 003 Parody 6 July 2022 1 min read

AA parody of the Chinese song Just Happened to Meet You (《刚好遇见你》), rewritten after a mathematics exam.

Yes, it was Guangya again.

We cried.

Guangya smiled.

We looked up at the maths exam.

Half the page was white.

We sang Guangya's song.

Only then we understood

Why every post-exam hug

Suddenly felt right.

Because I happened to meet you,

Red crosses bloomed so bright.

Sequences and derivatives

Kept me up all night.

Time escaped too quickly.

Variables wouldn't unite.

We left our hopes in July.

If we ever meet again...

It's on.

You.

And mathematics.
